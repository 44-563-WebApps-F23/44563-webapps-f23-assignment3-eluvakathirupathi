EDWARD TEACHER

Edward Teacher is a famous hair stylist in Kansas City. People Stand in queue for long hours to have hair cut in his saloon. Many celebrities come from far away to his saloon. Besides his own saloon Kansas, he also works for movies as a makeup person.

Physical/personality traits of Edward

  1. If he does any bad haircut, he calls it as fashion.
  2. Being a hair stylist, he is bald.
  3. He can change a person look new with his skills of hair style.
